Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Data We Collect

For workers: name, age, gender, skill, phone number, photo, and Aadhaar card (optional). For hirers: name and phone number. We also store your favourites and language preference on your device.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used only to display your profile to hirers and to allow hirers to contact you. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Data Storage

Currently all data is stored locally on your device. When the app connects to our server, data will be stored securely in our database with encryption.

Aadhaar Data

Aadhaar card uploads are used only for identity verification to add a Verified badge to your profile. We do not share Aadhaar data with anyone. We delete Aadhaar card photo immediately after verification.

Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process your request within 7 working days.
